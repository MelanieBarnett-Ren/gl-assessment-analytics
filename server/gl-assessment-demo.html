🎯 Integrated Assessment Insights

NGMT, PTM & CAT4 combined analysis - Baseline to attainment to progress

📚 CAT4Cognitive Abilities Test (4th Edition): Assesses reasoning abilities in four batteries to predict future academic performance and identify learning potential. (Cognitive Abilities Test)

Baseline assessment measuring cognitive potential

Measures: Verbal, Quantitative, Non-verbal, Spatial reasoningFour batteries: Verbal (language-based reasoning), Quantitative (number patterns), Non-verbal (diagrams/figures), Spatial (mental rotation/visualisation)

Use: Set targets, predict GCSE outcomes, identify potential

📊 NGMTNew Group Mathematics Test: Standardised assessment providing SAS scores, stanines, and percentiles to benchmark maths attainment against national norms. (New Group Mathematics Test)

Standardised maths assessment showing attainment

Measures: Number, Algebra, Shape/Space, Handling DataFour content domains covering all areas of the maths curriculum: calculations, equations, geometry, and statistics.

Use: Benchmark performance, identify skill gaps

📈 PTMProgress Test in Maths: Termly assessments with item-level data showing which specific skills students have mastered. Expected progress is +3 SAS per term. (Progress Test in Maths)

Termly progress monitoring with item-level data

Measures: Progress over time, specific skill masteryTracks SAS progress termly and provides item-level data showing exactly which questions students got right/wrong for targeted intervention.

Use: Track interventions, identify declining cohorts
